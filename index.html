About me

I am a passionate and creative Frontend Developer with over 1 year and 5 months of experience building responsive, user-friendly web applications. I specialize in crafting clean, functional interfaces using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Tailwind, React, and MUI, with a strong eye for detail and a commitment to delivering high-quality results.

My hands-on experience includes contributing to 98+ projects (both collaborative and individual) and leading the development of 30+ projects from concept to deployment.
